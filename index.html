Privacy Policy

Welcome to RealtyView, a real estate agent hub application developed by RealtyView. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information Collection and Use

RealtyView is designed to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of real estate agents. To provide a seamless experience, we collect and use certain information from you:

  • System Information: We collect system-related information such as device type, operating system, and application version to ensure compatibility and provide the best possible user experience.
  • Profile Picture: We require access to your camera and file storage to allow you to upload a profile picture. This picture will be used solely within the app to personalize your profile and make it easier for clients and colleagues to identify you.

We do not collect any other personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided by you within the app.

Permissions

To function correctly, RealtyView requires the following permissions:

  • Camera Permission: To take a photo for your profile picture.
  • File Storage Permission: To upload an existing photo from your device for your profile picture.

These permissions are used exclusively for the stated purposes and are not used to access any other information on your device.

Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your personal information, thus we are committed to using commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
